Our Church

At WWCC we seek to share the Good News of Jesus Christ, our Saviour and Lord, staying true to God’s Word, the Bible.


Sunday Services

Sunday Services are held in West Wyalong at 10:30am and at 9:00am at Ungarie.


Preaching

During Term 2 we will be focussing on the letter to the Philippians. We also have several guest preachers over this time.


Sunday School

Sunday School for Primary and Pre-School Children takes place during the second half of our Sunday morning services in West Wyalong during school terms.


Bible Study Groups

We have half a dozen mid-week Bible study groups (day-time and evening).


Youth Group

Various Activities and Studies for High Schoolers and Young Adults. Contact us for details.


Other Regular Activities

Young Mum’s Group (weekly), Prayer Meeting (monthly), Two Card Making & Scrapbook Groups (each monthly), Lunches / Dinners (occasional), Bonfire (annual).


Affiliation


We are an 'independent' church, operating as an incorporated association and affiliated with the Crosslink Christian Network.
